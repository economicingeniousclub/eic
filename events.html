Open Day

Open Day event, where we invite you to explore our club's departments and specialties within the prestigious School of Statistics and Applied Economy. This engaging day will feature an informative conference, a lively musical interlude, and a chance to showcase your talents with karaoke sessions. Participate in fun quizzes for a chance to win exciting gifts, and stay tuned for a surprise at the end of the event! We are thrilled to have other clubs joining us, creating a vibrant atmosphere of collaboration and community. After the Open Day, we will open the subscription link for those eager to become part of the EIC Club. Don't miss this opportunity to learn, connect, and celebrate with us!

Salon de l'Emploi et de la Formation des grandes écoles

The highly anticipated Salon d'Emploi event organized at the highest School of Statistics and Applied Economy in Kolea, Algeria, the largest event of its kind hosted by our club! This is an incredible opportunity for anyone seeking employment or internships, featuring comprehensive training sessions on project management and CV creation. Attendees will also benefit from engaging conferences on current topics such as recent trends in labor statistics and startup creation. Leading up to the day of the company exposition, participants will have the chance to expand their professional networks through direct interactions with industry experts. With +60 exhibiting companies across various sectors, including 6 actively recruiting, this event is designed to connect you with potential employers and guide you toward securing a job or internship effectively. All under the high patronage of the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research, as well as the Ministry of Digitalization and Statistics. Salon de l'Emploi Sponsored by 5 prominent companies and in partnership with Heetch. Don't miss this chance to elevate your career prospects and make valuable connections in the job market!

EIC Games Tournament

Game Tournament, designed specifically for students looking to break free from the study routine after their first semester exams! This exciting event features an incredible lineup of games that will put your skills to the test, including classic favorites like Monopoly, Uno, and Ludo, as well as thrilling options like VR gaming, archery, chess, dominoes, and a treasure hunt. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or just looking to have some fun, this tournament promises a fantastic opportunity to unwind, connect with fellow students, and showcase your competitive spirit. Join us for a day filled with laughter, camaraderie, and unforgettable memories as we celebrate the joy of gaming together!

OpenData

Open Data event, where the brightest minds in data science will come together for a thrilling competition! Participants will tackle a real-world problem presented by a leading company, putting their skills to the test in a race against the clock. Over the course of 23 hours, we will provide valuable training sessions covering the fundamentals of data science, data visualization, manipulation techniques, and classification strategies. Attendees will also benefit from insightful conferences and hands-on workshops to refine their solutions and perfect their pitches for the final presentation day. With support from our esteemed partners Yassir, Gomycode, and Canon, along with sponsorship from five dynamic companies, participants will have the resources they need to succeed. To ease any pre-competition nerves, we’ll also incorporate ice-breaking activities. Exciting prizes await the winners, making this not just a competition but an incredible opportunity for learning, collaboration, and innovation in the data science domain. Don’t miss out on this chance to showcase your talents and network with industry professionals!

AlgeriELLE

Algerielle event, a vibrant celebration dedicated to honoring the valuable contributions of women in our society. This inspiring gathering features a diverse array of exhibitors showcasing their talents in various fields, including art, pottery, cheese-making, painting, candle-making, embroidery, and crochet. Attendees can participate in engaging workshops focused on pottery, crochet, and painting, allowing them to explore their creativity and learn new skills. Additionally, we have arranged a thought-provoking discussion panel featuring successful women from different sectors, sharing their insights and experiences. To add to the festive atmosphere, enjoy a lively musical interlude and a spectacular show that promises to bring joy and laughter. We are proud to have Gaia Event Planner as our sponsor, making this event a truly memorable occasion for all. Celebrate the strength and creativity of women with us at Algerielle!

Campeco

Campeco event, a dynamic national competition in the field of economics that unfolds over just 23 hours! Partnered with Dusens Group, we present a unique challenge that encourages participants to tackle real-world problems while enhancing their skill sets. Attendees will benefit from inspiring conferences led by industry experts and practical workshops designed to elevate their competencies. This is a one-of-a-kind opportunity to network with professionals and enthusiasts alike through engaging panel discussions. With the support of five sponsors, our program includes training sessions on audience analysis, marketing techniques, idea formalization, public speaking, and the business model canvas. The event features four esteemed judges and culminates in an entertaining show, complete with a musical break that sets the perfect atmosphere. Don't miss out on this exceptional opportunity to learn, connect, and shine!
