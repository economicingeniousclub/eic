Our departments

EIC fosters growth and collaboration across 4 dynamic departments: Marketing & Communication, Human resources, Finance & Logistics and External Relations

Human Ressources

Join our Human Resources team dedicated to enhancing workplace well-being and fostering team spirit. We focus on evaluating performance and achieving common HR goals to support a thriving and cohesive work environment.

  • Empathy: Ability to understand and address employee needs and concerns.
  • Evaluation Skills: Proficiency in performance assessment and feedback processes.
  • Team Building: Experience in organizing activities that strengthen team cohesion.
  • Communication: Clear and effective in conveying policies and supporting employees.
  • Conflict Resolution: Skills in managing and resolving workplace issues.
  • Organizational Skills: Ability to manage HR processes and track employee well-being initiatives.
  • Supportive Attitude: Commitment to fostering a positive and inclusive workplace culture.

Marketing Department

Join our team to engage in creative storytelling and community management. We offer roles in video editing, design, and community management, each requiring specific skills to help us connect with and captivate our audience.

  • Creativity: Essential for crafting compelling video content and innovative designs.
  • Technical Proficiency: Familiarity with tools such as CapCut, Premiere Pro, Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Figma, and Canva.
  • Attention to Detail: Crucial for producing polished video edits and precise design work.
  • Graphic Design Skills: Understanding of design principles and software.
  • Creative Thinking: Ability to develop unique and resonant content.
  • Time Management: Efficiently handle multiple projects and meet deadlines.
  • Strong Interpersonal Skills: Engage effectively with a diverse audience.
  • Adaptability: Flexibility in managing community engagement and feedback.

External relations

The External Relations unit is a key pillar of our club, dedicated to building strong connections with the outside environment and creating enriching opportunities for students and members.

Its goal is to link the club with key players in the professional, academic, and associative worlds, offering students unique experiences, diverse resources, and an expanded network.

The unit is organized into three sub-groups:

  • The Company Visits Group organizes company visits and educational outings, allowing members to explore professional environments, discover different careers, and meet experienced professionals.
  • The Sponsors, Collaborators, and Partners Group focuses on establishing relationships with sponsors and partners, developing strategic collaborations, negotiating advantageous partnerships, and ensuring financial support for the club’s activities.
  • The Trainers and Speakers Group organizes training sessions and conferences by inviting experts, opinion leaders, and professionals.

Finance and Logistics

Join our Finance and Logistics team, where we manage logistics, secure agreements, and ensure smooth operations. Our focus is on optimizing financial processes and logistical coordination to support our organization’s goals.

  • Financial Acumen: Expertise in managing budgets, financial planning, and cost control.
  • Logistical Coordination: Skills in organizing and managing supply chains and operational logistics.
  • Negotiation Skills: Proficiency in securing agreements and managing vendor relationships.
  • Organizational Skills: Ability to handle multiple tasks and ensure efficient workflow.
  • Attention to Detail: Ensuring accuracy in financial records and logistical arrangements.
  • Problem-Solving: Capability to address and resolve issues related to logistics and finance.
  • Communication: Effective in coordinating with internal teams and external partners.
