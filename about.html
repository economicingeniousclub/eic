About Economic Ingenious Club

Discover our story, our mission, and the values that drive us forward.

Our story

Learn about our story

Founded in 2018 at the National Higher School of Statistics and Economics, the Economic Ingenious Club was born from the vision of a group of passionate and driven students. United by a shared love for economics, data, and innovation, they sought to create a vibrant community where like-minded individuals could explore new ideas, collaborate on projects, and gain real-world insights into the ever-evolving economic landscape.

From humble beginnings, the club quickly grew into a hub of creativity and learning, offering workshops, seminars, and competitions that challenge members to think critically and solve complex problems. Our mission is to empower the next generation of economists, statisticians, and data scientists by fostering a culture of collaboration, curiosity, and excellence.

Join us as we continue to inspire and shape the future of economics!
