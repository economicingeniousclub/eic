Frequently Asked Questions

How can I determine which department needs my skills?

Consult our departments page to understand their roles and functions and than apply for membership.

Are events open to non-participants?

Some events are open to the public, while others are exclusive to participants. Check every event details for specific information.

Who is eligible for membership?

Membership is open to all skilled students from diverse fields who can contribute value to EIC.

How can I apply for membership?

Interested students can apply by completing the membership application form available on our website or Instagram when registrations are open at the beginning of the academic year.
